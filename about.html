Our Story

Learn more about Whipped by Apeksha

I’m Aadone Apeksha — an 18-year-old dreamer, creator, and connector from a proud Marathi family of four. Currently, I’m shaping my future through a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) at Avinash College, where I combine academic knowledge with real-world ambition.

What began as a simple pastime — baking — has evolved into my greatest passion and a calling I’m determined to turn into a thriving career. Each creation that comes out of my oven is a reflection of my love for the craft, my attention to detail, and my drive to spread happiness, one delicious bite at a time.

Beyond baking, I’m deeply social at heart. I thrive on conversations, exchanging ideas, and building genuine connections. Meeting new people, understanding diverse perspectives, and actively engaging with my community isn’t just something I enjoy — it’s what energizes and inspires me every day.

Welcome to my world — where passion meets purpose, and every moment is a chance to create something extraordinary.
