The Story Behind Whipped

Welcome to Whipped by Apeksha, a home bakery born from a passion for creating delicious treats that bring joy to every occasion.

What started as a hobby has blossomed into a beloved local bakery, serving the Hyderabad community with freshly baked cakes, cupcakes, and desserts made with love and the finest ingredients.

Every creation is handcrafted in small batches to ensure quality and freshness, bringing the authentic taste of homemade goodness to your celebrations.
